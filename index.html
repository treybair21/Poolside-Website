About Us

How the Process Works

Selling your pool route doesn't have to be complicated. Our streamlined process guides you from first conversation to successful sale.

Introduction Call

We start with a quick call to understand your route — account count, revenue, service mix, and geography. We provide a ballpark valuation and outline the best path forward.

Valuation & Listing Prep

We price your route using current market comps and adjust for density, delinquency, and repair potential. Our approach aligns with PHTA industry standards for accurate valuations.

Buyer Sourcing & Offers

We tap into our active buyer network and run targeted outreach to vetted operators. Every buyer is pre-screened for funding and experience, with most sellers seeing offers within 1–2 weeks.

Close & Transition

We guide you through contracts, escrow, and payment. We then support the transition with customer notifications, billing handover, and training for both parties.
