About Us

How the Process Works

Selling your pool route doesn't have to be complicated. Our streamlined process is designed to guide you every step of the way — from the first conversation to a successful sale. We connect sellers with qualified buyers, manage the details, and make sure the transition is smooth and stress-free.

Introduction Call

We start with a quick call to understand your route—account count, revenue, service mix, and geography. With just a simple export, we provide a ballpark valuation and outline the best path forward. This ensures you know what to expect before committing to the process.

Valuation & Listing Prep

We price your route using current market comps and adjust for density, delinquency, and repair potential. Our approach aligns with PHTA industry standards to ensure accurate valuations. From there, we create professional marketing materials tailored to your route—from concise route briefs to detailed buyer packets with KPIs, maps, and transition planning.

Buyer Sourcing & Offers

We tap into our active buyer network and run targeted outreach to vetted operators and consolidators. Every buyer is pre-screened for funding and experience, and most sellers see real offers within 1–2 weeks. We manage negotiations to maximize price and secure seller-friendly terms.

Close & Transition

Once an offer is accepted, we guide you through contracts, escrow, and payment, ensuring a secure close. We then support the transition with customer notifications, billing handover, and training/ride-alongs, giving both seller and buyer confidence that the accounts will transfer smoothly.
