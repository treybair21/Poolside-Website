Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a pool route cost?

Pool routes typically sell for 12 times the monthly service revenue. For example, a route generating $10,000/month would sell for $120,000. The multiple depends on account density, retention, and market demand.

Can I finance a pool route purchase?

Options include seller financing, equipment loans, and specialized lenders who work with route-based businesses. We can connect you with financing resources.

What if I've never serviced pools before?

Many successful route buyers start with no pool service experience. The seller provides hands-on training, and we can connect you with additional educational resources.

How quickly can I start after purchase?

Most buyers begin servicing accounts within 1-2 weeks of closing. This allows time for training, equipment setup, and customer notifications.

What are the ongoing costs of running a route?

Main costs include chemicals, equipment maintenance, vehicle expenses, and insurance. Pool routes are known for their strong profit margins.

What size route should I buy?

If you're starting as an owner-operator, most buyers begin with 40-80 accounts. If you plan to hire help, larger routes of 100+ accounts work well.
